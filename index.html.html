    About me

    A Visual Storyteller

    "WHY I CHOOSE DESIGNING?"

    I’ve never really had a career in any other field or seriously considered another field of study. It's a very thoughtful, challenging profession. Designers are creative problem solvers, so each brief represents a fresh challenge. You see, every industry in the world benefits from good design, so there is a need for designers in virtually any field you can imagine. I have a lot of curiosity, and I knew as a graphic designer, I would have the opportunity to work with a lot of different clients and get to learn about their work without having to be an expert in their field myself. There are always new trends, new tools, new technologies, new theories, new sources of inspiration – a creative mind never stops learning. So this is Me - Would love to know about everyone..."
